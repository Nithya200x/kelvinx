Next-Generation Heating & Cooling for Tomorrow's Homes

At Kelvinix, we are developing a next-generation heating and cooling system designed for the homes and buildings of tomorrow.

Traditional HVAC systems are bulky, energy-hungry, and expensive to run. Our solution takes a different approach: compact, modular, and highly energy-efficient, tailored for the unique needs of Irish and European housing.

  • Reduce energy consumption compared to conventional units
  • Lower household bills while maintaining year-round comfort
  • Simplify installation with a modular “plug-and-fix” design
  • Integrate with retrofit and green building programs

Currently in the feasibility and prototyping phase, our product is supported by Irish innovation funding and backed by global partnerships. With each step, we are moving closer to market readiness — bringing a smarter, greener way to heat and cool homes.

This is more than a product. It’s the beginning of a new standard in sustainable comfort.
