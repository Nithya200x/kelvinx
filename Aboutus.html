Driving Sustainable Building Innovation

We create climate-smart HVAC solutions powered by renewable energy and AI — making buildings efficient, green, and future-ready.

Our Mission

To make high-efficiency, low-carbon HVAC solutions the standard — not the exception.

2023: Inspiration

Kelvinix was born out of a simple belief: comfort and sustainability should go hand in hand.

2024: The Challenge

Heating and cooling consume enormous energy, leading to high bills and heavy carbon footprints. We saw an opportunity to change that.

2025: Founded in Ireland

Backed by global partners, Kelvinix began reimagining climate control for real homes and businesses—not just test labs.

2025: Growth & Partnerships

From feasibility studies with Irish innovation funding to partnerships with sustainability leaders, our movement accelerated.

Future

We're shaping a future where energy efficiency is effortless and comfort is cleaner, smarter, and more accessible.
